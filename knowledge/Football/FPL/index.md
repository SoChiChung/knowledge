# Fantasy Premier League (FPL)

Fantasy Premier League is the official fantasy football game of the English Premier League. Millions of players worldwide compete each season.

## Basic Rules

- Budget: £100 million for 15 players
- 2 goalkeepers, 5 defenders, 5 midfielders, 3 forwards
- 1 free transfer per gameweek
- Captain scores double points, vice-captain is backup

## Chip Strategy

| Chip | Effect | Best Use |
|------|--------|----------|
| Wildcard | Unlimited transfers | Overhaul team |
| Free Hit | One-week team reset | Blank gameweeks |
| Bench Boost | Bench players score | Double gameweeks |
| Triple Captain | Captain × 3 | Double gameweeks |

## Stats to Watch

Expected goals (xG), expected assists (xA), and minutes played are key indicators. Don't chase last week's points — look at underlying numbers.

## Mini-Leagues

Create or join mini-leagues to compete with friends. Head-to-head leagues offer a different challenge from classic scoring.
