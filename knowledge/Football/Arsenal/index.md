# Arsenal FC

Arsenal Football Club is a professional football club based in Islington, North London, England. They compete in the Premier League, the top tier of English football.

## History

Founded in 1886, Arsenal has won 13 league titles, a record 14 FA Cups, and numerous other trophies.

## The Invincibles

The 2003-04 season saw Arsenal go the entire Premier League campaign unbeaten — a feat unmatched in the modern era. The squad, led by manager Arsene Wenger, earned the nickname "The Invincibles."

## Emirates Era

Since moving to the Emirates Stadium in 2006, the club has entered a new phase of growth under Mikel Arteta, with a focus on young talent and attractive, possession-based football.

## Key Players (2024-25)

- **Bukayo Saka** — Homegrown star, elite winger
- **Martin Odegaard** — Captain and creative heartbeat
- **Declan Rice** — Midfield powerhouse
- **William Saliba** — Defensive rock
